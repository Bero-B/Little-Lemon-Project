Popular Choices

Iskender Kebab (Turkish)

Thin slices of grilled lamb, topped with tomato sauce served over pita bread. The dish also includes melted sheet butter and yogurt.

Gyro (Greek)

Pieces of meat (chicken, pork, lamb or beef) cooked on a rotisserie and wrapped in a flatbread along with salad, onions and a variety of sauces.

Pasta Carbonara (Italian)

One of the most popular pasta dishes made with eggs, a combination of Pecorino Romano cheese and parmesan, cured pork, and black pepper.
